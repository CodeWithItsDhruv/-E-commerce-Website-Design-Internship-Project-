Shipping Policy

Information about our shipping methods, delivery times, and costs

Shipping Methods

At ShopEase, we offer various shipping options to ensure your orders reach you in the most convenient way possible. Our shipping partners are carefully selected to provide reliable and efficient delivery services.

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Delivery 3-5 business days ₹49 (Free above ₹499)
Express Delivery 1-2 business days ₹99
Same Day Delivery Within 24 hours ₹199 (Select cities only)

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to most locations across India. Delivery times may vary based on your location:

  • Metro Cities: 2-3 business days
  • Tier 2 Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • A shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • Real-time tracking updates via SMS
  • Access to detailed tracking information on our website

Shipping Restrictions

Please note the following restrictions:

  • Certain items may not be eligible for express or same-day delivery
  • Some remote areas might have limited delivery options
  • Hazardous materials have special shipping requirements
  • International shipping is currently not available

Important Note

Delivery times are estimates and may be affected by factors such as weather conditions, public holidays, or other unforeseen circumstances. We will keep you informed of any significant delays affecting your order.
