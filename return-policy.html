Return Policy

Easy returns within 30 days of delivery

Return Process

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery.

  • Initiate Return: Log in to your account and go to your orders. Select the item you want to return and click on 'Return Item'.
  • Select Reason: Choose the reason for return from the dropdown menu and provide additional details if necessary.
  • Schedule Pickup: Select a convenient date for pickup. Our delivery partner will collect the item from your address.
  • Package Item: Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all tags and labels intact.
  • Refund Processing: Once we receive and verify the returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following criteria:

  • Item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • Item must be in the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Return must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Item must not be part of the non-returnable category
  • You must have the receipt or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

  • Intimate wear and lingerie
  • Personal care products
  • Cosmetics and beauty products (if opened)
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Perishable goods
  • Downloadable software products

Refund Information

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund according to the following timeline:

Payment Method Refund Time Additional Info
Credit/Debit Card 5-7 business days Refunded to original card
UPI/Net Banking 3-5 business days Refunded to source account
Store Credit Immediate Added to your account

Exchange Policy

If you'd like to exchange your item for a different size or color:

  • Exchange must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • Exchange is subject to product availability
  • You can exchange an item only once
